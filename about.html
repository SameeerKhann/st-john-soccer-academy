About the Academy

A competitive, respectful, growth-oriented environment where young athletes thrive — on and off the pitch.

Our Mission

Building players. Shaping people.

St. John Soccer Academy was founded on a simple belief: every child deserves coaching that takes their potential seriously. We combine a professional training curriculum with the warmth of a family club — where standards are high, effort is celebrated, and every player is known by name.

Success for us isn't only measured in trophies. It's the shy six-year-old who now sprints onto the pitch. The twelve-year-old whose weaker foot became a weapon. The sixteen-year-old who leads the warm-up and shakes the referee's hand. That's development.

What We Stand For

The values behind every session

R

Respect

For teammates, opponents, officials, and the game. Zero tolerance for referee abuse — many officials are young people learning the trade, just like our players.

G

Growth

Mistakes are part of learning. We coach through encouragement and honest feedback, and we measure players against their own progress.

C

Community

Parents, players, and coaches pulling in the same direction. We win and lose with class, together.

E

Excellence

Planned sessions, punctuality, proper kit, full effort. Professional habits build professional futures.

Leadership

Meet Coach Johannes

Founder and Technical Director of St. John Soccer Academy, Coach Johannes built the academy around one promise to parents: let the coaches coach, and we will treat your child's development as our profession — not a hobby.

Every St. John coach is licensed, background-checked, and trained to deliver the same curriculum with the same standards, so your player gets a consistent, professional experience at every session.

  • Licensed coaching staff

    Nationally certified coaches with youth-specific training.

  • Safety first

    Background checks, first-aid trained staff, and clear supervision protocols at every event.

  • Open communication

    Scheduled parent meetings and term progress reports for every player.
