Ages 4–7

Little Saints

Fun-first sessions built around games and imagination. Players develop balance, coordination, and basic ball mastery while learning to love the sport. Parents welcome pitch-side at every session.

Youth Development

Our core academy program. A periodized curriculum covering first touch, dribbling, passing patterns, finishing, and small-sided game intelligence — with individual progress feedback each term.
