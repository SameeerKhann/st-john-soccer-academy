Welcome to the Academy

More than training.
A home for your player.

Our mission is to foster a competitive, respectful, and growth-oriented environment where young athletes thrive both on and off the pitch. Every session is planned, purposeful, and age-appropriate — delivered by coaches who care about the person as much as the player.

  • Proven development pathway

    A structured curriculum that takes players from first touch to competitive team soccer.

  • Small-group coaching ratios

    Every player gets real touches, real feedback, and real attention every session.

  • Character comes first

    Respect for teammates, opponents, and officials is coached as deliberately as technique.

The St. John Method

Four pillars of player development

Every training block is built on the same four pillars, adapted to each age group and level.

01

Technical Mastery

Thousands of quality touches every week — first touch, dribbling, passing, striking, and 1v1 moves.

02

Game Intelligence

Scanning, decision-making, and positional understanding trained through small-sided games.

03

Athletic Development

Age-appropriate speed, agility, balance, and strength work to build durable, explosive athletes.

04

Character & Leadership

Discipline, respect, and resilience — the habits that make great teammates and great people.
