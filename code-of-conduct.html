Parent Agreement &
Code of Conduct

Welcome to the St. John Soccer Academy family. Your attitude, behavior, and support are vital to your child's development and the integrity of our Academy.

Our mission is to foster a competitive, respectful, and growth-oriented environment where young athletes can thrive both on and off the pitch. By enrolling your child, you agree to uphold the following standards throughout the 2026/27 season.

1Sideline Etiquette & Sportsmanship

Support, Don't Coach.

We ask that you cheer positively for all players. Please refrain from shouting instructions, tactical advice, or commands from the sideline. Duplicate instructions confuse the players and conflict with the Academy coaches' technical plans.

Respect the Officials.

St. John Soccer Academy maintains a zero-tolerance policy for referee abuse. Respect all match officials and their decisions, keeping in mind that many are youth referees learning the trade.

Respect the Opposition.

Treat opposing players, coaches, and spectators with dignity. We compete fiercely but always win and lose with class.

Let the Coaches Coach.

Trust the Academy's training curriculum. Avoid undermining the coaching staff's authority or decisions in front of your child or other parents.

2Communication & The 24-Hour Rule

The 24-Hour Rule.

If you have a question or concern regarding playing time, positioning, or game strategy, you agree to wait 24 hours after the match before contacting the coach. This ensures conversations are calm, objective, and constructive.

Appropriate Settings.

Discussions regarding player development should happen during scheduled, private meetings — never immediately before, during, or after a match or training session.

Player Ownership.

For older age groups, we strongly encourage players to advocate for themselves and approach the coaching staff directly with questions about their development.

3Commitment & Logistics

Punctuality.

Ensure your player arrives at the field dressed and ready at the designated arrival time — 15 minutes before training and 45 minutes before matches.

Attendance Tracking.

Use the approved Academy communication tool (WhatsApp / TeamSnap) to update your child's availability for all sessions and matches at least 24 hours in advance.

Gear and Uniforms.

Ensure your child attends every Academy event in the appropriate, required St. John Soccer Academy training gear or match kit.

Acknowledgement. Registration includes agreement to this Code of Conduct. Repeated or serious breaches may result in a meeting with Academy leadership and, in rare cases, suspension of sideline privileges. Thank you for helping us protect the environment our players deserve.
